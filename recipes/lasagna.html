Meat Lasagna

When you're done cooking, it should look as delicious as this

Ingredients

  • Meat: For veggie versions, you can use soy meat
  • Onion and Garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic
  • Tomato: A can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce and two cans of tomato paste
  • Sugar:Two tablespoons
  • Spices and seasoning:fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, ricotta
  • Egg: Helps binding the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna

Step-by-Step

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving

How to Layer Lasagna

Layer the lasagna in the following order:

  1. Meat Sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat Sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat, then top with parmesan

How to cook the Lasagna

The assembled lasagna should take about 50 minutes to cook in a oven preheated to 375 degrees F.
